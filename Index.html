At-home movie nights have become more and more frequent over the past year given the current world pandemic. With so many streaming services, it’s difficult to choose what to watch. We are faced with cognitive impairment, struggling to make a choice due to the overload of options out there. Groovy Movie will solve that for you! Simply input what movie genre and decade you are interested in, and Groovy Movie will select 5 movie titles randomly that fit your criteria. Once the 5 films are selected, click on the movie poster and see a brief synopsis and the rotten tomato rating. Found more than one recommendation you’d like to watch? Save movie titles to your list to revisit at a later time!
