With so many streaming services, it’s difficult to choose what to watch. We all struggle with the overload of options out there. Groovy Movie will help reduce the number of options for you! Simply input what movie genre and release year and Groovy Movie will select 5 movie titles that fit your criteria. Once the 5 films are selected, click on the movie poster to see more details or click the "Save to My List" button for later. Found more than one recommendation you’d like to watch? Save movie titles to your list to revisit at a later time!
